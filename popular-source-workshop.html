Psyc 408 Human Behavior Genetics

Week 13 Popular Source Workshop

Participation Activities

  • Provide feedback on others’ draft popular sources
    • Follow the survey link posted in Moodle, which will show you a set of popular source drafts for your feedback. They are organized into four “blocks” that will be presented in random order. Within each block, you will be shown (a random subset of) 8 popular source drafts.
    • Each (unique) completed block earns one participation point. At the end of each block, you will be asked if you would like to continue or stop. You can work through all of the blocks in a single session, or you may complete one (or a few) and return later - however, please keep track of which blocks you complete each time, so you can skip the ones you’ve already completed the next time you log in. The blocks are labeled: Celadon, Phlox, Vermilion, and Rainbow Unicorn.
  • Journal Response: Reflections on others’ drafts
    • Answer both parts of all four questions.
      • What did you see that you LIKED in the popular source drafts that you provided feedback on? How will that inform your revision of your popular source or your paper?
      • What did you see that you did NOT like in the popular sources that you provided feedback on? How will that inform your revision of your popular source or your paper?
      • What did you see that you LIKED in the paper drafts that you are peer-reviewing? How will that inform your revision of your popular source or your paper?
      • What did you see that you did NOT like in the paper drafts that you are peer-reviewing? How will that inform your revision of your popular source or your paper?
